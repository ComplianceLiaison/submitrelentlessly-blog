We're Streamlining the Submission Process

Written by Compliance Liaison · May 2, 2025

Thanks for Your Feedback!

We’ve heard your comments loud and clear: you want faster access, easier copying, and fewer barriers. So we’re working on it.

Starting now, you’ll notice that you can copy and paste submissions directly from the website — no PDFs, no downloads, no formatting drama. Just click, copy, and submit.

As we continue releasing new content, we’ll also be rolling out site-wide updates over the next few days. If something doesn’t look quite perfect yet… that’s because it isn’t. But we’re getting there. Thanks for sticking with us while we build this out and upload more ready-to-go deregulation suggestions. We appreciate your patience—and your support—as we keep refining things behind the scenes.

Our goal is simple: provide as many strong, smart, ready-to-submit ideas as possible — so you can copy / paste / submit / repeat.

Let’s keep pushing.

Compliance Liaison
Department of Deregulation
